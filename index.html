Definition of a triangle

A triangle is a plane figure with three straight sides and three angles. We have three types of triangle, which are;

EQUILATERAL

A triangle that has all length sides equal

ISOCELES

A triangle that two sides have the same length

SCALENE

A triangle that has three unequal sides length
